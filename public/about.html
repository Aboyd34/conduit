Why Conduit exists

Every major platform today is built on one assumption: that your identity, your behavior, and your data are assets to be collected and monetized by someone else.

Conduit is built on the opposite assumption. Your identity is yours. It lives in your browser, not on a server. Your posts are signed and relayed — the relay never knows who you are.

No accounts. No email. No password. No profile that someone else owns. Just communication.

How it works

  • Your identity is a cryptographic keypair generated locally in your browser on first visit.
  • Posts are signed with that key before transmission. The relay sees a signed packet, not a name.
  • Keys live in your browser's local storage — not on any server or database.
  • Clearing your browser removes your local identity. Back it up yourself in the You panel.
  • The relay is infrastructure, not a record keeper.
